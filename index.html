Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar, water, cardamom, and saffron to make syrup.
    Add rose water and simmer.
  2. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee, and milk to form a soft dough.
    Rest and shape into balls.
  3. Fry the balls in hot oil or ghee until golden brown.
  4. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for 2-3 hours.

Burfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Mix 2 cups of milk powder, 1/2 cup of sugar, and 1/2 cup of ghee in a pan.
  2. Heat the mixture on low flame, stirring continuously until it thickens.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of milk and cook until the mixture forms a soft dough.
  4. Spread the mixture on a greased tray, let it cool, and cut into squares.
